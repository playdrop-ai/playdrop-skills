# Gameplay Review

Use this when deciding whether a build is actually strong enough to ship or relist.

## Review checklist

- the first 15 seconds create desire to keep playing, not just understanding
- the core loop is immediately legible
- controls and signifiers are readable without extra prose
- the strongest raw screenshot or moment already looks worth clicking
- the build supports a real session and a reason to replay
- the gameplay fantasy, audience, and public promise still line up
- the live build is not depending on listing art to rescue a weak core

## Output

Produce:

- a review verdict
- blocking issues
- non-blocking polish issues
- the next best handoff: plan, scope, improve, test, or list
